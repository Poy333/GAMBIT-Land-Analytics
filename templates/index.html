About GAMBIT

GAMBIT (Geographic Analysis and Mapping of Barangay Irrigated Territories) aims to support farmers in identifying suitable agricultural lands in mountainous regions through advanced geographic data analysis and mapping techniques.

Our Goals

Land Mapping

Identify the usability of agricultural land with geographic data to enhance farming practices in mountainous barangays.

Soil Science

Analyze soil quality and recommend improvements to increase crop yields and soil health in mountainous regions.

Farmer Support

Provide tools, resources, and expert advice to farmers to improve productivity and sustainability.

Helping Farmers Allocate Suitable Agricultural Lands

We assist farmers in identifying lands that are suitable for farming and those that are not, ensuring effective and sustainable agricultural practices in mountainous regions.
