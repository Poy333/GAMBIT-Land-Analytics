ABOUT GAMBIT

GAMBIT is an initiative designed to enhance the sustainable development of agricultural lands in the mountain barangays of Cebu. Through advanced geographic analysis and mapping, we aim to identify and promote usable agricultural lands while addressing soil quality concerns. Our project is focused on empowering local farmers with the knowledge and tools they need to optimize land use for greater productivity and sustainability.

By mapping out irrigated territories and studying land usability, GAMBIT provides insights that enable farmers to make informed decisions about land management, crop selection, and soil improvement. We also work closely with local authorities and experts to improve the quality of farming practices across these regions.

OUR VISION

At GAMBIT, our vision is to create a sustainable, thriving agricultural ecosystem in the mountain barangays of Cebu. We envision a future where every piece of land is utilized to its fullest potential, with farmers equipped with the knowledge, resources, and support they need to improve their livelihoods.

Our goal is to identify lands that are suitable for farming, while also recognizing areas with poor soil quality, so that farmers can avoid unproductive areas and focus on more viable, fertile land.
